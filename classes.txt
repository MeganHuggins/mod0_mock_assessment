Class: Dog

Attributes:
color (string)
breed (string)
currentWeight (integer)
furLength (integer)
isGoodBoy (boolean)
tricksKnown (array)

Methods:
feedDog (adds 1 to currentWeight)
learnTrick (adds a trick at the end of tricks array)
walkDog (subtracts 1 from currentWeight)
petDog (Change isGoodBoy to true)
groomDog (subtracts one inch from furLength)
